<h5>What is this?</h5>
<p>This metric tests to see if you have an ad blocker installed, and is either ‘True’ or ‘False,’ and may differ depending on <em>how</em> you block ads. </p>
<h5>How is this used in your fingerprint?</h5>
<p>This adds little information to your fingerprint, but can be minimally useful in making you identifiable.</p>
