<h5>What is this?</h5>
<p>A plugin is a small piece of software that helps a browser manage content it is unable to process on its own. </p>
<h5>How is this used in your fingerprint?</h5>
<p>Browser plugins have been phased out over the past few years. Instead, browsers favor more regulated add-ons and extensions. But plugins persist in older browsers.</p>
